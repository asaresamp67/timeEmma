Fatal is not for children of God

Posted on July 7, 2020July 8, 2020 by Emmanuel Opoku

Mystery of Exemption

I fate no one in the eyes of God. We count not be fated. For a man be fated is by his conscience and choice. God had given us the power and authority (freedom)to choose, however, He (GOD) ordered us to choose well.

Almost 80% of man’s calamity, disaster, fatal etc. The results of his reasoning, carelessness, wrong choice, compromising faith (little faith) and fear etc. Fatalism is a root of faithless (No faith in God). In Deuteronomy 30:19, God told Israel through his servant Moses that” I call heaven and earth to record this day against you; I have set you “life and death” blessing and cursing”; therefore, choose life.

My question is why is that God didn’t end at the “cursing”, or God didn’t say “choose the one you want “but God Himself ordered us to choose life? ANS; This means, God wants good for us. He had given us the power to choose, but it is His willing for us to choose life (good things, eternal joy).

God had no pleasure, He gains nothing in the perished of unbeliever or sinners, His will is for us to choose His way and repent from our sins. God hates the fatality (death) of all people, God wants life and eternal life. (Ezekiel 18:23, 18:32, 33:11, 2Peter 3:9, 1Timothy 2:4).

All that God wants us to run to Him and choose His ways. God had no pleasure in our death, but He can’t do anything about it because He had given us the power to choose life or death and He continued to admonish us to choose life. So, it is up to us to choose life, joy, love, peace and He will give us the grace to pursue all.

God had every power and authority to force us to choose His ways, but He is justice and faithful God that He had given us freedom, the power to choose and He support us if we choose good or eternal life (joy).

Lost turn from our careless, wrong choice, less faith, pain, evil ways and boldly draw near the grace throne of God so that we will live and walk in the status of life, joy, prosperity, peace, love etc. Ezekiel 18:31” cast away all your transgression, whereby you have transgressed; and make you a new heart and a new spirit; for why will ye die? Matthew 11:28 Jesus said, “come unto me “all who labor is heavy-burden and overburdened in this contest refers to our sins that we have tried several times, but we cannot stop, when we move to Jesus, He will cause us rest.

Read Ephesians 4:22-24,1corinthians 5:17,1Peter 5:7 cast all your anxieties on Him. Ezekiel 33:15. If the wicked restore the pledge, give again that he had robbed, walked in the status of life, without committing iniquity, he shall live and walk-in status of life. What is walking “status of life?

Grace abounds you everywhere you locate, peace, love, faith, joy, pure, hope, trust, hope, humility, prosperity is your portion, that is walking in the status of life.

This verse 16 continues says none of his sins that he had committed shall be mentioned unto him again. What a powerful declaration from God, glory. Let us walk boldly and draw near the throne of grace of God, that we may obtain mercy and find grace to help in time of need. Hebrew 4:16. Two things involved here

  1. May obtain mercy 2. Find grace. Exposition “May “there is condition. Romans 9:14-18. For the “Lord” have mercy on whom He wants you can’t find mercy but you can’t find grace. But as for “grace” it is given unto us free, Jesus Christ had paid for it already about 2000 years ago.
  2. All that we must do is to locate grace and find it. BIG QUESTION BUT SIMPLE ANSWER. One will ask what about such circumstances as; birth problem (miscarriages), situations in life (accident, nations economy) international events government policies and many more over which we have no control of them?
  3. What about that? The answer is simple; opposing circumstances should create a firm determination to overcome them for those who trust in God. God does not depend on the things in this world before He blesses His children.
  4. The breaking down of one barrier will give one greater ability and courage to move forward. Press with determination in the right direction and circumstances will be your helpers, not your hindrance. Let remember what Paul said in Romans 8:31 “If God is with us, who can be against us” as 1Timothy 1:7 illustrated, He gave us the spirit bold, love and a sound mind. We are no more us (your own life) but we are Christ who lived in us. For the one who lived in us is bigger than the whole earth, Galatians 2:20. Christ in you the hope of glory.

As we press forward in faith and trust in God, we should purpose our mind that we are now accompanied by God and His promises towards us. Below is some God promise toward us.

(1) Jeremiah 29:11>He knows the thought he had for us.

(2) Psalm 91:1-11>great weapon read.

(3) Psalm 23:1-6>greater promises and declaration.

(4) Isaiah 54:16-17>no weapon formed against us shall prosper.

(5) Job 36:11>spend days in prosperity.

(6) 1John 1:2.

(7) Philippian 4:19>My Lord will supply all our needs.

(8) Psalm 27:1…….

(9) Proverbs 10:3.

PRAYER; Lord God, I thank you for this revelation about death and fatal. As your word promises us, may we desire more grace to overcome any fatal in our life. Jesus had overcome fatal already and He dwelled in us, therefore, no fatal with have dominion on us.in Jesus name. AMEN!!!!
